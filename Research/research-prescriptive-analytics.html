Prescriptive Analytics & Decision Support

A focused research stream within AI Foresight Lab.

← Back to Research

Overview

This stream applies prescriptive analytics and decision-support methods to business and organizational problems. It connects analytics models with practical decision-making in uncertain and technology-driven environments.

Methods

  • Optimization and mathematical programming
  • Simulation and scenario analysis
  • Decision analysis and value-of-information reasoning
  • AI-supported decision systems

Focus

The research emphasizes actionable insights, structured decision-making, and the translation of analytical models into managerial recommendations.
