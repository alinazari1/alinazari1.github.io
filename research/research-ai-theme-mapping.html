AI Theme Mapping

A focused research stream within AI Foresight Lab.

← Back to Research

Overview

This research maps AI-related themes across Entrepreneurship, Information Systems, and Technology Management. It uses expert-informed keyword categories to interpret how different research communities discuss AI, adoption, capability, technology, and entrepreneurial opportunity.

Method

The approach groups keywords into AI Technology, AI Capability, AI Adoption, and AI Entrepreneurship categories. Category scores are then calculated for each document and visualized using clustering, network analysis, multidimensional scaling, and t-SNE.

Contribution

  • Provides an expert-informed method for interpreting AI research landscapes.
  • Compares AI-related themes across Entrepreneurship, IS, and TIM.
  • Supports technology intelligence and research mapping activities.
