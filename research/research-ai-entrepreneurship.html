Overview

This research examines how AI and analytics can support entrepreneurship, innovation, opportunity recognition, business model design, and venture decision-making.

Research Questions

  • How can AI help entrepreneurs recognize and evaluate opportunities?
  • How can analytics support business model development and validation?
  • How can technology intelligence improve entrepreneurial decision-making?

Applications

Applications include venture design, customer discovery, value proposition analysis, market intelligence, and evidence-based opportunity assessment.
