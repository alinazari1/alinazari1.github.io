Technology Foresight & Intelligence

A focused research stream within AI Foresight Lab.

← Back to Research

Overview

This stream focuses on technology scanning, technology intelligence, and technology foresight. The goal is to help researchers and organizations identify emerging trends, interpret weak signals, and understand how technologies evolve over time.

Method

The research combines text analytics, topic modeling, expert input, similarity mapping, and visualization to support systematic exploration of technological landscapes.

Applications

  • Emerging technology monitoring
  • Research landscape analysis
  • Strategic technology planning
  • Innovation and policy intelligence
